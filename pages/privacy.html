Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. Learn how we handle and protect your global business data.

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

At EverFresh International, we operate with transparent standards. When you visit our website, enquire about products, or engage in international trade, we may collect the following data:

  • Personal Branding Details: Name, job title, and company name.
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, and WhatsApp communication details.
  • Export Requirements: Port of destination, specific product interests (Spices, Grains, Fruits, etc.), and volume requirements.

2. How We Use Data

The information gathered helps us streamline international logistics and optimize your commercial trade workflow. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Generate accurate global market price quotes and commercial invoices.
  • Respond efficiently to your product enquiries submitted via our website forms or email.
  • Provide updates regarding global phytosanitary certifications, shipment vessel schedules, and transit statuses.

3. Global Data Security

We implement robust, state-of-the-art administrative and digital security infrastructure to safeguard your sensitive trade operations. Your trade contracts, import-export configurations, and communication logs are heavily guarded against unauthorized internal or external access.

We strictly do not rent, sell, or compromise your trade identity or contact details to third-party marketing companies.

4. Cookies and Web Analytics

Our website utilizes minor analytical cookies to enrich user accessibility. These cookies safely observe overall traffic patterns and session drops, helping us adapt our user interface, product filter speeds, and global regional load times to better serve your device.

5. Contact & Regulatory Enquiries

If you have any doubts, regulatory queries, or require immediate corrections concerning the retention of your commercial metadata records, feel free to reach out to our legal compliance desk.
