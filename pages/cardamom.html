Cardamom

Origin: Western Ghats

Sourced From High-Altitude Kerala & Western Ghats

EverFresh International LLP sources premium-grade Green Cardamom (Elettaria cardamomum) directly from the lush, high-altitude plantations of Idukki and the Western Ghats. Known as the "Queen of Spices," our cardamom pods are harvested at perfect maturity and cured under temperature-controlled setups to retain their vibrant natural green color, heavy weight, and intense exotic aroma notes demanded by luxury global markets.

The Analytical Size & Volatile Oil Matrix

Every batch passes through precise mechanical sieve-graders and color-sorting lines to categorize pods by millimeter thickness. Our quality assurance processes ensure that light pods, open pods, and thrips-damaged skin are completely eliminated, leaving only plump, seed-filled pods rich in essential oils like cineole and limonene.
